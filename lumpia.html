LUMPIA SHANGHAI

"yummerz" -Homelander


Ingredients:
  • 1 package of lumpia wrappers
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/3 cup onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/3 cup ground pork (finely chopped)
  • 1/3 cup carrot (finely chopped)
  • 1 qt oil (for frying)

Procedure:
  1. Lay several wrappers on a clean and dry surface. Cover with a damp towerl to prevent wrappers from drying out.

  2. Mix the beef, pork, onion, pepper, and carrot together in a medium bowl. Place 2 tbsp of the mixture along the center of the wrapper and fold one edge of the wrapper over filling. Roll it into a closed cylinder and seal it with a little water. Repeat with other wrappers.

  3. Heat oil in a frying pan (Medium to medium-high heat) and fry the lumpia wrappers until browned. Drain excess oil using paper towels.

  4. Serve the lumpia with a sweet chilli sauce and enjoy!
