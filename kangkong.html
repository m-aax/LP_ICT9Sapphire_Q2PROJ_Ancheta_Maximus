CRISPY KANGKONG

"yummerz wowie" -Homelander


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups oil
  • 2 pcs egg
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup breading mix
  • 4 cups of kangkong leaves

Procedure:
  1. Mix the eggs, cornstarch, and the breading mix in a bowl.

  2. Coat the kangkong leaves evenly with mixed batter.

  3. Fry the battered leaves in oil at around a temperature of 175 Celsius until crispy.

  4. Serve it with mayonnaise and enjoy!
