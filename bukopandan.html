BUKO PANDAN

oh yeah


Ingredients:
  • 20 oz young coconut strips
  • 250 ml all-purpose cream
  • 150 ml condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup cooked sago pearls (optional)

    • For gelatin:

  • 2 cups water
  • 3 oz powdered gelatin
  • 1/2 lb pandan leaves
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp buko pandan flavoring

Procedure:
  1. Boil water in a saucepan, then add pandan leaves and cover, continuing to boil for 15 minutes. Remove the leaves afterwards.

  2. Add sugar, powedered gelatin, and buko pandan flavoring, stirring until the power is completely dissolved in water.

  3. Turn off the heat and transfer the mixture to a mold, allowing it to cool until firm. For quicker results, chill in the refrigerator.

  4. Combine young coconut strips, condensada, all-purpose cream, and cooked sago pearls in a bowl. Chill in the refrigerator or freezer for a few hours until the texture thickens.

  5. Slice the firm gelatin into 1/2-inch cubes. Combine the gelatin cubes with the creamy mixture of cream, sago pearls, and condensed milk.

  6. Transfer to individual serving platters or cups, and optionally top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

  7. Serve and enjoy your buko pandan!
