TAPSILOG

"too tasty"


Ingredients:

    For Tapa:

  • 1 lb beef sirlion sliced thinly
  • 3 pcs eggs
  • 6 tbsp cooking oil

    • For marinade:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 6 cloves of garlic (crushed)
  • 3/4 cups pineapple juice
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper

    • For Garlic Rice:

  • 5 cups of leftover rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 5 cloves of garlic (crushed)

Procedure:
  1. Marinate the beef in a large bowl with the soy sauce, garlic, pineapple juice, brown sugar, and white pepper. (Highly recommend to marinate overnight!)

  2. Cook the garlic rice by heating 3 tbsp of oil in a pan. Add the garlic until light brown, then add the leftover rice. Season with salt, and stir-fry for 4-6 minutes. Afterwards, set it aside.

  3. Start to cook the tapa. Heat a pan and pour the marinated beef into it, including the marinade. Add Â¾ cups water. Let the mixture boil. Cover the pan and continue to cook until the liquid reduces to half. Add 3 tablespoons cooking oil into the mixture. Continue to cook until the liquid completely evaporates. Fry the beef tapa in remaining oil until medium brown. Set aside.

  4. Fry the egg by pouring 1 tablespoon oil on a pan. Crack a piece of egg and sprinkle enough salt on top. Cook for 30 seconds. Pour 2 tablespoons water on the side of the pan. Cover and let the water boil. Continue to cook until the egg yolks gets completely cooked by the steam.

  5. Arrange the beef tapa, sinangag, and fried egg on a large plate to form Tapsilog. Serve with vinegar as dipping sauce for tapa.
