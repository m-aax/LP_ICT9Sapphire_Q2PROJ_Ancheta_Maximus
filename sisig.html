SISIG

🤤🤤🤤🤤oh my gawd🤤🤤🤤🤤🤤🤤🤤


Ingredients:
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 1 whole pork snout
  • 1 pig ear
  • 1/2 cup chicken liver
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 12 garlic cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • peppercorns
  • sili labuyo (chopped)
  • 1/3 cup onion (chopped)
  • 1 egg (for topping)
  • 1 sisig seasoning packet
  • 2 kalamansi

Procedure:
  1. Combine pork belly, snout, ears, along with the vinegar and soy sauce ub a stock pot. Fill with water until meat is covered. Add bay leaves, salt, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil then simmer until meat is no longer tight and rough.

  2. Remove meat from pot and grill for 10 mins.

  3. Chop pork belly and face meat into 1/4-1/2 inch cubes. Finely chop the pig ears.

  4. Mix chopped pork in a bowl then add the chillis, kalamansi juice, vinegar, salt, pepper, and the sisig seasoning packet.

  5. In a pan over medium heat, add oil and saute shallots for 1-2 mins. Add chicken livers; mash and saute until cooked through.

  6. Add chopped pork (with liquids) to the pan until meat is hot.

  7. (OPTIONAL) Heat a cast iron platter, add a bit of oil, and add the sisig to make it sizzling. Then, serve and enjoy!
